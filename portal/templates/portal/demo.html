Our Mission

We are taking a data driven approach to pain management to help physicians and patients review opioid usage and reported symptoms over time to produce an optimal treatment plan which can include opioid therapy, physical therapy, and counseling to reduce pain and reduce harmful side effects.

PICARD

PICARD (Patient initiated controlled analgesic recording dispenser) stores, records and dispenses potentially abused and highly misused prescription opioid medication securely and prompts users to enter their pain score after a certain period of time after pill dispensation to help track the efficacy of the prescription opioid regimen.

Our Prototype

PICARD is a smart device that targets drug abusers by using biometric sensor, auto-locking technology and real time data recording.

Biometric Sensor

Sensing the right patient and right time, it will dispense the correct dosage to the patient.

Auto-Locking

Each device is linked to a unique PICARD online account with patient's prescription, through which physicians, pharmacists, and/or care givers can note the patient's pain level and monitor his/her medication routine.

Touch Screen

The touch screen on the top displays a step-by-step instructions, allowing easier device-human interactions.

Real Time Data

Real time data such as patient's conditions, pill dispensing status, and excessive attempt of accessing pill can be transmitted through WiFi/cellular internet and other smart technologies.

Hygienic Container

With a compact pill container, the medication can be kept in a sterile environment. It is disposed of and replaced once pills are used up for hygienic purposes.

Data Sharing

When the pills are nearly depleted, dispensing mechanism with pill count technology notices and informs patient and physician.
